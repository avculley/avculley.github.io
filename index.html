Welcome!

My name is Alexis Culley and I am a physical environmental science PhD student in Dr. Maya Stoke's lab at Florida State University. I graduated with a Masters of Science in Biology in December of 2023 from Austin Peay State University. My past and current research focuses on how the surrounding landscape may impact population connectivity and distribution of freshwater fishes in the Southeast and Appalachian regions of the United States.

Come check out and explore my webpage to hear more about my prior eduction, experiences, and past/ongoing research!
